Real value through C1 Rewards

Included in each Member package is a certain amount of free C1 Rewards.

When you earn Streamline Bonus, Educational Package Sales, Matching Bonus and Fear of Loss bonus, a part of the bonus will be converted to C1 Rewards.

Each Crowd1 Reward will represent a value equal to the total value to be distributed among Affiliates divided by the number of available Crowd1 Rewards. When you happen to receive Crowd1 Rewards as part of your bonus this will be generated by an auto-buy at the exchange rate of 1 Crowd1 Reward equals 20BP (1 BP = 0.1€).

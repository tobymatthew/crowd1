Fear of Loss
Bonus

Fear of Loss Bonus is a quick-start motivational bonus that Crowd1 offers all new Affiliates during their first 14 days.

Rules and more info
  • The 14 day timer starts when you sign up.
  • You must have paid for your package. If you do not hold a package you will instead of the bonus earn your own White EducationalPackage (without BP).
  • The amount you get to keep is 31,500 BP if you have 4 fully paid Titanium packages, 10,050 BP if you have 4 fully paid Gold packages, 3,750 BP if you have 4 fully paid Black packages and1,250 BP if you have 4 fully paid White packages. 
  • If there is a combination of packages or amounts paid* for each the 4 packages you will get 31,5% of 4 times the amount that is paid for the package that has the fourth-highest amount.
  • Example 1:
    2 fully paid Black packages and 2 fully paid White packages, the lesser bonus will be paid, in this example 1,250 BP will be what you are qualified to keep.
  • Example 2:
    3 fully paid Titanium and 1 Titanium that has been paid for 5,990BP (1 fully paid White package generating 990 BP, plus free upgrade to Black package, plus paid upgrade to Gold package generating 5,000BP, plus free upgrade to Titanium package). In this example 7,550 BP (4 x 5,990 = 23,960 x 31.5% = 7,550) will be what you get.
    *Only the amount that has been fully paid entitles you for Fear of Loss Bonus. If parts of the Education Package have been paid with Gift Vouchers without BP or gained by free upgrades, these parts do not qualify for Fear of Loss Bonus.
